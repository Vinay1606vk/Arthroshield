Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ARTHROSHIELD

What is ARTHROSHIELD and how does it work?

ARTHROSHIELD is an AI-powered orthopedic care platform that analyzes X-rays using advanced machine learning algorithms. Our system provides accurate diagnoses, severity assessments, and personalized treatment recommendations by combining cutting-edge technology with expert medical oversight.

Is my medical data secure and private?

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade encryption and HIPAA-compliant security measures to protect your medical information. All data is stored securely, and only authorized healthcare professionals can access your records with your explicit consent.

Is ARTHROSHIELD available worldwide?

Yes, ARTHROSHIELD is available globally. Our platform supports multiple languages and complies with international medical standards. However, specialist availability may vary by region, and some features require local medical licensing.

What certifications and approvals do you have?

Our platform is FDA-approved, CE-marked for European markets, and certified under ISO 13485 for medical devices. We maintain strict compliance with healthcare regulations including HIPAA, GDPR, and local medical device regulations.

How can doctors join ARTHROSHIELD?

Medical professionals can join by completing our verification process, which includes license verification, credential checks, and a brief platform training. The onboarding process typically takes 2-3 business days. Start by clicking "Sign Up" and selecting "Doctor" as your role.

What specialties are supported?

We support orthopedic surgeons, radiologists, sports medicine physicians, physical therapists, and general practitioners with orthopedic focus. Our AI is specifically trained for musculoskeletal conditions and bone-related diagnostics.

How does AI assist in diagnosis?

Our AI provides preliminary analysis, highlights potential issues, measures angles and distances, and suggests severity grades. You maintain full clinical control - the AI serves as an advanced assistant that saves time and improves accuracy while you make the final diagnosis.

Can I integrate with my existing EMR system?

Yes! We offer seamless integration with major EMR systems including Epic, Cerner, and Allscripts. Our API allows for automatic patient data sync, result import, and streamlined workflow integration. Custom integrations are available for specialized systems.

How do I get paid for consultations?

Doctors receive payments through secure bank transfers within 7 days of completed consultations. We handle billing, insurance claims, and payment processing. Rates vary by specialty and case complexity, with top specialists earning $150-300 per review.

How do I create a patient account?

Creating an account is simple and takes just 2 minutes. Click "Sign Up", select "Patient", and provide basic information including your name, email, and date of birth. You'll need to verify your email and can then immediately start using our services.

How do I upload my X-rays?

You can upload X-rays directly through our secure portal. We accept DICOM files, JPEG, PNG, and PDF formats. Simply drag and drop your files or use the upload button. For best results, ensure images are clear and properly oriented. Maximum file size is 50MB per image.

Can I schedule appointments with doctors?

Yes! After your X-ray analysis, you can schedule virtual consultations with board-certified orthopedic specialists. Video calls are available within 24-48 hours, and in-person appointments can be arranged at partner facilities in your area.

How do I access my medical reports?

All your medical reports are stored securely in your patient portal. You can view, download, or share them with other healthcare providers. Reports include AI analysis, specialist reviews, treatment recommendations, and follow-up instructions.

Is there a mobile app for patients?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. You can upload X-rays, view results, schedule appointments, track recovery progress, and communicate with your care team. The app includes push notifications for important updates and reminders.

Can I track my recovery progress?

Absolutely! Our platform includes comprehensive recovery tracking tools. You can log symptoms, track pain levels, monitor mobility improvements, and record physical therapy progress. Visual charts help you and your doctors track your healing journey over time.

What are the pricing plans?

We offer three main plans: Basic ($49 per analysis), Professional ($99/month for 5 analyses), and Enterprise (custom pricing). Annual subscriptions save 20%. All plans include AI analysis, specialist review, and secure storage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For enterprise clients, we also offer invoicing and bank transfer options. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your payment details on our servers. All transactions are processed through PCI-compliant payment processors. We're also GDPR and CCPA compliant for data protection.

Do you provide invoices for insurance claims?

Yes, we provide detailed invoices with CPT codes and medical documentation for insurance claims. Invoices include provider information, service dates, and diagnosis codes. Most major insurers cover our services under telemedicine benefits.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service, request a refund within 30 days. For monthly subscriptions, you can cancel anytime and continue using the service until the end of your billing period.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we're in-network with major insurance providers including Blue Cross, Aetna, UnitedHealth, and Cigna. We can submit claims directly to your insurance. Check with your provider about coverage for AI-assisted diagnostics and telemedicine services.

What are the system requirements?

Our web platform works on all modern browsers (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+). Mobile apps require iOS 13+ or Android 8+. Minimum internet speed: 5 Mbps for uploads, 2 Mbps for general use. We recommend 8GB RAM for optimal performance.

How is data stored and backed up?

All data is stored in secure, HIPAA-compliant cloud servers with automatic hourly backups. We maintain redundant copies across multiple geographic locations. Data retention: 7 years for medical records, 2 years for financial data. Export options available anytime.

What integrations are available?

We integrate with major EMR/EHR systems (Epic, Cerner, Allscripts), PACS systems, lab information systems, and billing platforms. API access available for custom integrations. HL7, FHIR, and DICOM standards supported.

How do you handle system downtime?

We maintain 99.9% uptime with automatic failover systems. Scheduled maintenance announced 48 hours in advance. Real-time status available at status.arthrosheild.com. Emergency protocols ensure critical services remain operational.

What are your terms of service?

Our terms govern use of the ARTHROSHIELD platform, including service availability, user responsibilities, payment terms, and dispute resolution. Key points: 30-day notice for service changes, binding arbitration for disputes, compliance with applicable laws.

How does HIPAA compliance work?

We are HIPAA Business Associates with comprehensive compliance programs. All protected health information is encrypted, access is logged, and we maintain Business Associate Agreements with all partners. Regular audits and staff training ensure ongoing compliance.

What about medical liability?

Our AI provides decision support, not medical diagnosis. Licensed healthcare professionals make final clinical decisions. We maintain comprehensive malpractice insurance and clearly define scope of practice. Users agree to telemedicine terms during onboarding.

International compliance and regulations?

We comply with GDPR (Europe), PIPEDA (Canada), HIPAA (US), and local medical device regulations. Data processing agreements available for international clients. Localization options for language and regulatory requirements.

What are your support hours?

24/7 emergency technical support for critical issues. Business support: Monday-Friday 6 AM-8 PM EST, Saturday-Sunday 8 AM-6 PM EST. Average response time: 2 hours for priority issues, 24 hours for general inquiries.

What training resources are available?

Comprehensive onboarding program with video tutorials, documentation, and live training sessions. Certification courses for advanced features. Monthly webinars on best practices. Dedicated customer success managers for enterprise clients.

How do I report technical issues?

Report issues via in-app support ticket, email support@arthrosheild.com, or call 1-800-ARTHRO. Include screenshots, error messages, and steps to reproduce. Critical issues automatically routed to engineering team with 30-minute response SLA.

What communication channels are available?

Live chat (website and app), email support, phone support, video calls for complex issues, community forum, and dedicated account managers. Priority support channels available for premium plans with guaranteed response times.
