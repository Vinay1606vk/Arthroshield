Information We Collect

At ARTHROSHIELD, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Medical history and records
  • X-ray images and diagnostic reports
  • Treatment plans and progress data
  • Account credentials and login information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our medical services:

Primary Uses

  • X-ray analysis and diagnosis
  • Treatment plan creation and monitoring
  • Progress tracking and reporting
  • Communication with healthcare providers

Secondary Uses

  • Service improvement and research
  • Customer support and assistance
  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption: All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption

Secure Storage: Medical records stored in HIPAA-compliant servers

Access Control: Role-based access to sensitive information

Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

Information Sharing

We only share your information in specific circumstances:

When We Share

  • With your explicit consent
  • With healthcare providers involved in your treatment
  • When required by law or regulation
  • To protect against fraud or illegal activity

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request and review your information
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your account and data
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Opt-out: Decline certain data collection and marketing

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Posting updates on our website
  • In-app notifications for logged-in users
